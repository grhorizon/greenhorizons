What Is Green Horizons?

Green Horizons is a resource to help homeowners learn about green and clean energy that they can use in their homes. It's our goal to lower the effects on the environment through using more green energy in residential houses. We support Carbon Pollution-Free electricity (CFE) which is electricity produced through forms like wind, solar, geothermal, and nuclear power. Green Horizons aims to empower homeowners with knowledge of how to lessen their carbon footprint and save more energy. Green energy initiatives are important to help the environment and slow down the negative impact that fossil fuel emissions have on the planet.
